Requirements

To be considered for our incubation program, applicants should meet the following criteria:

  • Innovative and scalable business idea
  • Commitment to full-time startup development
  • Team with complementary skills (preferred)
  • Willingness to participate in training programs
  • Clear problem-solution fit
  • Market potential and target audience
